	- make several suggestions about ...
	- *suggest some improvements about ...*
	- complain about

- 咨询/自荐
	- *inquire sth about 咨询*
	- recommend ... to you

## 💫通用
> 衔接词
- To begin with
- Besides / Moreover / Secondly
- Last but not least / most significantly


## 💫建议信
> 第一段
- 介绍自己 + 建议
	- I'm Li Ming, a student of our university...(同位语, 自我介绍)
	- I'm writing to give you some suggestions about ...
- 回应 + 建议
	- Knowing that you have trouble in ..., I'm writing to
	- Knowing that you are busy with ...
- 祝贺 + 建议
	- *Congratulations on your admission to college.* I know that you worked so hard for this in the past days, and all the effort was worth it. At the same time, *I'm delighted to know that you want to seek some suggestions about ... from me.*
- 欢迎 + 建议
	- On behalf of Student Union of our university, I would like to extend our cordial welcome to you. In order to help you ..., I would like to give you some suggestions.

> 第二段
- To begin with, it will be easier for you to 主题 if you can ...
- Besides, ... can make a difference to your ...
- Last but not least, ... is necessary in order to ...

> 第三段通用
- I will be grateful if you can take my suggestions into consideration. I'm looking forward to your early reply.
- I hope you will find these proposals helpful. If you still have other questions about this matther, please feel free to contanct me by email.

> 落款
- Yours sinserely / respectfully
