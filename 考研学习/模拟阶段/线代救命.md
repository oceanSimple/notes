# ♾️矩阵计算
## 💫行列式
$$
\begin{align}
|kA| &= k^n|A|
\end{align}
$$


## 💫转置
$$
\begin{align}
A^T - B^T &=(A-B)^T \\
|A| &=|A^T| \\
(kA^T) &= kA^T \\
(AB)^T &= B^TA^T
\end{align}
$$

## 💫逆矩阵
$$
\begin{align}
(kA)^{-1} &= \frac{1}{k}A^{-1} \\
(A^T)^{-1} &= (A^{-1})^T \\
A^{-1} &= \frac{1}{|A|}A^* 
\end{align}
$$

## 💫伴随矩阵
$$
\begin{align}
AA^* &= A^*A = |A|E \\
|A^*| &= |A|^{n-1} \\ 
(kA)^* &= k^{n-1}A^* \\
A^* &= |A|A^{-1} \\
|A^*|^* &= |A|^{n-2}A
\end{align}
$$
